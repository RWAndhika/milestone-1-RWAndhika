The Kumon Method

Individualized Instruction

Allows each student to study at a level that is best suited to his or her academic ability, regardless of age or school grade, and advance beyond his or her current school grade level.

Self-Learning

The ability to study proactively makes it possible to pursue one’s dreams and goals.

Small-Step Worksheets

Kumon’s small-step worksheets enable all students to advance on their own.

Kumon Instructors

Kumon Programs

Mathematic

Daily Kumon math practice takes about 30 minutes to complete. Students attend class twice a week (tuesday and thursday) at Kumon MRC and complete assignments at home the other five days. Every child works at their own pace and masters each new math concept before moving forward. You will watch your child’s math skills advance and see their confidence grow.
